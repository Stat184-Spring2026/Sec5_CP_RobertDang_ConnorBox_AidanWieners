# What Makes a Good Free Throw Shooter?

This repo will display our data visulisations and findings while exploring if there are certain attributes of an NBA player that have a high correlation with their free throw percentage rate in the 2025/2026 regular season.

## Overview
We are exploring if a player's height, wingspan, free throw attempts, and position have any real relationship with their FT%. 
To keep things cleaner, we only looked at players who took at least 150 free throws this season. 

### Interesting Insight (Optional)

As average player height goes up across positions, free throw percentage tends to go down. Guards usually have the highest FT% while centers have the lowest. The bubble size in the plot shows how many players are at each position.

## Data Sources and Acknowledgements

Our data was collected from databallr.com, and was manually collected and compiled into an excel spreadheet by our team to be saved as a csv file.

## Current Plan
Our current plan with this project is to figure out if certain player attributes, especially height and position, are related to how well NBA players shoot free throws. 
We’re using out findinngs to see if taller players tend to have lower free throw percentages, if guards shoot better than centers, and how much free throw attempts actually matter.
